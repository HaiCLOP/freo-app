Product OverviewFreo MUN is a vertical sub-platform of Freo — India's first MUN-native conference management system. It handles the complete lifecycle of a Model UN conference: from organizer onboarding and committee creation, through delegate registration and UPI payment, AI-powered portfolio allotment, pre-conference submission workflows, live committee session management, awards, and automated certificate generation."Freo MUN is what happens when a person who has actually sat in a crisis committee builds the software — not a generic ticketing platform with a MUN skin on top."1.1 The Core Design PhilosophyEverything is dynamic — no hardcoded committee types, country lists, or flowsOrganizers define their own universe — UN bodies, Indian Parliament committees, fictional (F1 paddock, Hogwarts Council, Marvel Senate)Roles are granular — Sec-Gen, USG, Director, Chair, Vice-Chair, Rapporteur, Delegate, Observer, PressAI does the painful work — portfolio allotment, preference matching, conflict detectionIndia-first infrastructure — UPI screenshot verification, INR pricing, WhatsApp notificationsWorks standalone 1.2 Who Uses Freo MUNUser TypeWho They ArePrimary Pain Point SolvedSecretary-GeneralStudent running the whole conferenceManaging 20+ committees, 500+ delegates with zero tech budgetUSG / DirectorCommittee head overseeing 1–3 committeesCoordinating allotments, reviewing position papers, tracking delegate progressChair / Vice-ChairRunning the live committee roomSpeakers list, voting, directives, notes — all on one screenDelegateSchool/college student attending the conferenceRegistering, paying, knowing their portfolio, submitting position paper, tracking awardsObserver / PressNon-delegate attendee or MUN journalistGetting access without going through delegate registration flowFaculty AdvisorTeacher/professor overseeing the conferenceVisibility into their school's delegates, payments, and performance⚙️  2. ORGANIZER FLOWS3.1 Conference Setup FlowThe Sec-Gen creates and configures the conference. This is the master flow everything else hangs off.01Create Conference AccountSec-Gen signs up on Freo MUN. Creates organization profile: conference name, logo, year, host institution, social links.02Conference SettingsSet dates (registration open/close, conference days), delegate fee (free or paid), max delegates, city/venue, visibility (public/private).03Add CommitteesUse the Dynamic Committee Engine. For each committee: pick type, set name, define portfolios, configure EB roles, upload background guide PDF and for each committee background guide(can be uploaded even 1 hr before the MUN) .04Configure Registration FormDrag-and-drop custom fields on top of defaults (name, email, school, phone). Add fields like: experience level, dietary restrictions, accommodation needed, preferred committees, allergies.05Set Payment ConfigFree event OR paid. If paid: set amount (INR), accepted payment methods (UPI manual screenshot /)Set refund policy.06Publish & ShareConference gets a unique Freo MUN URL (freomun.in/[conf-slug]). One-tap share to WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn. QR code auto-generated for posters.07Invite EB MembersSec-Gen sends invite links to Directors/Chairs. They join and are assigned to their committee. Internal EB — no registration fee.3.2 Registration Management Flow01Delegate AppliesFills registration form, selects committee preferences (ranked 1–3), uploads payment screenshot (if paid).02Organizer Reviews DashboardSec-Gen / USG sees all pending applications in a Kanban-style view: Pending → Under Review → Approved / Rejected / Waitlisted.03Payment VerificationUPI screenshot shown alongside transaction UTR. Organizer marks as Verified / Suspicious. Future: AI-assisted verification flags.04Approve or RejectOne-click approve: Freo MUN auto-sends confirmation email + QR ticket + committee assignment placeholder. Reject: auto-sends rejection email with optional reason.05Bulk ActionsSelect multiple delegates → Bulk approve, bulk reject, bulk export to Google Sheets. Filter by school, payment status, committee preference.06Waitlist ManagementAuto-fills approved spots from waitlist when a delegate cancels. Notifies next person on waitlist automatically.3.3 AI Portfolio Allotment FlowThe most painful part of running a MUN — manually matching 300 delegates to 40 committees considering preferences, experience, school caps, and conflicts. Freo MUN automates this with AI.01Input PhaseRegistration closes. System has: all delegate profiles, committee preferences (ranked), experience level, school, any manual locks set by Sec-Gen.02Sec-Gen Sets Allotment RulesConfigure: max delegates per school per committee, preference weight (how much to honor 1st choice), experience distribution balance, manual locks (specific delegate → specific portfolio).03AI Runs AllotmentGroq / Gemini API runs constraint-satisfaction optimization: maximize preference scores, enforce school caps, balance experience levels, detect conflicts (two delegates from same school in same committee).04Review Allotment OutputSec-Gen sees full allotment table. Stats shown: % got 1st preference, % got 2nd, conflicts found. Can manually override individual allotments.05Finalize & NotifyConfirm allotment. System sends each delegate their portfolio via email + updates their Freo MUN delegate dashboard.06Re-run if NeededCancellations after allotment? Re-run partial allotment for unfilled spots only, keeping confirmed allotments locked.3.4 Position Paper Management Flow01Sec-Gen Opens Submission WindowSets deadline per committee. Delegates can upload position papers (PDF/DOCX) through their dashboard.02Director / Chair ReviewsCommittee EB gets a review queue. For each paper: Accepted / Accepted with Comments / Rejected. Add inline comments visible to delegate.03Delegate Revises & ResubmitsIf revision requested, delegate can resubmit. System tracks version history.04Position Paper ScoresOptional: EB can score papers (1–10) for Best Position Paper award tracking.3.5 Awards & Certificates Flow01EB Submits Award NominationsA Marking management system like execl where marksheet can be created using custom fields and stuff or just upload the marksheet from execl files. Post-conference, each committee's Chair submits award picks: Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, Verbal Mention, Special Mention, Best Position Paper.02Sec-Gen Reviews & ApprovesCross-committee view. Confirm awards, resolve duplicates.03Auto-Generate CertificatesSystem auto-fills certificate template with: delegate name, conference name, committee, portfolio, award type, date, Sec-Gen signature field.04Bulk Download + EmailSec-Gen downloads all certificates as ZIP. Delegates receive their certificates via email + downloadable from their dashboard(optional) because mostly awards and certificates are given physically.05Participation CertificatesEvery delegate (awarded or not) gets a participation certificate automatically. No more manual bulk generation(optional).🎓  4. DELEGATE FLOWS4.1 Delegate Registration Flow01Discover ConferenceDelegate gets link from WhatsApp / Instagram / school notice board. LIVE COMMITTEE SESSION ENGINE6.1 Session ModesFreo MUN's native session engine supports the core MUN procedures. For conferences wanting advanced features, it partners with a committee flow tool.FeatureNative Freo MUN CapabilityRoll CallDigital attendance marking. One-click present/present & voting/absent per delegate. Quorum auto-calculated.Speakers ListGeneral speakers list — add/remove delegates, auto-countdown timer per speech, queue management.Moderated CaucusMotion raised → Chair approves → Total time + speaking time set → Speakers list opens → Timer runs.Unmoderated CaucusTimer only. Delegates free to caucus. System records caucus duration in session log.Voting ProcedureIn-favour / Against / Abstain per delegate. Automatic pass/fail calculation. P5 veto logic for UNSC.Directives (Crisis)Delegates submit directives from their device. Crisis Director reviews in Crisis Panel. Accept / Modify / Reject.Crisis UpdatesCrisis Director publishes update → appears on all delegate screens + Chair screen simultaneously.Working PapersDelegates co-author working papers. Submit to Chair. Chair publishes to committee.ResolutionsWorking papers promoted to draft resolution. Voting procedure triggered.Session LogAuto-generated transcript: motions raised, outcomes, speakers, timings. Downloadable post-session.6.2 Joint Crisis Committee (JCC) ArchitectureJCC is two linked committees running simultaneously. Actions in Committee A create consequences in Committee B. This is the hardest flow to manage — Freo MUN handles it natively.Each JCC side has its own Chair, delegates, and speakers listCrisis Directors have cross-committee visibility — can inject updates to both sidesCross-committee directives: a delegate in Committee A sends an action that affects Committee BCrisis arc is shared — both committees progress through the same story stagesSec-Gen has a JCC overview dashboard — sees both rooms simultaneously🗄️  7.
