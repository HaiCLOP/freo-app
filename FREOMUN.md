Product OverviewFreo MUN is a vertical sub-platform of Freo — India's first MUN-native conference management system. It handles the complete lifecycle of a Model UN conference: from organizer onboarding and committee creation, through delegate registration and UPI payment, AI-powered portfolio allotment, pre-conference submission workflows, live committee session management, awards, and automated certificate generation."Freo MUN is what happens when a person who has actually sat in a crisis committee builds the software — not a generic ticketing platform with a MUN skin on top."1.1 The Core Design PhilosophyEverything is dynamic — no hardcoded committee types, country lists, or flowsOrganizers define their own universe — UN bodies, Indian Parliament committees, fictional (F1 paddock, Hogwarts Council, Marvel Senate)Roles are granular — Sec-Gen, USG, Director, Chair, Vice-Chair, Rapporteur, Delegate, Observer, PressAI does the painful work — portfolio allotment, preference matching, conflict detectionIndia-first infrastructure — UPI screenshot verification, INR pricing, WhatsApp notificationsWorks standalone1.2 Who Uses Freo MUNUser TypeWho They ArePrimary Pain Point SolvedSecretary-GeneralStudent running the whole conference"Managing 20+ committees, 500+ delegates with zero tech budget"USG / DirectorCommittee head overseeing 1–3 committees"Coordinating allotments, reviewing position papers, tracking delegate progress"Chair / Vice-ChairRunning the live committee room"Speakers list, voting, directives, notes — all on one screen"DelegateSchool/college student attending the conference"Registering, paying, knowing their portfolio, submitting position paper, tracking awards"Observer / PressNon-delegate attendee or MUN journalistGetting access without going through delegate registration flowFaculty AdvisorTeacher/professor overseeing the conference"Visibility into their school's delegates, payments, and performance"⚙️ 2. 🏛️ 3. ORGANIZER FLOWS3.1 Conference Setup FlowThe Sec-Gen creates and configures the conference. This is the master flow everything else hangs off.01Create Conference Account"Sec-Gen signs up on Freo MUN. Creates organization profile: conference name, logo, year, host institution, social links."02Conference Settings"Set dates (registration open/close, conference days), delegate fee (free or paid), max delegates, city/venue, visibility (public/private)."03Add Committees"Use the Dynamic Committee Engine. For each committee: pick type, set name, define portfolios, configure EB roles, upload background guide PDF and for each committee background guide(can be uploaded even 1 hr before the MUN) ."04Configure Registration Form"Drag-and-drop custom fields on top of defaults (name, email, school, phone). Add fields like: experience level, dietary restrictions, accommodation needed, preferred committees, allergies."05Set Payment Config"Free event OR paid. If paid: set amount (INR), accepted payment methods (UPI manual screenshot /)Set refund policy."06Publish & Share"Conference gets a unique Freo MUN URL (freomun.in/[conf-slug]). One-tap share to WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn. QR code auto-generated for posters."07Invite EB MembersSec-Gen sends invite links to Directors/Chairs. They join and are assigned to their committee. Internal EB — no registration fee.3.2 Registration Management Flow01Delegate Applies"Fills registration form, selects committee preferences (ranked 1–3), uploads payment screenshot (if paid)."02Organizer Reviews DashboardSec-Gen / USG sees all pending applications in a Kanban-style view: Pending → Under Review → Approved / Rejected / Waitlisted.03Payment VerificationUPI screenshot shown alongside transaction UTR. Organizer marks as Verified / Suspicious. Future: AI-assisted verification flags.04Approve or RejectOne-click approve: Freo MUN auto-sends confirmation email + QR ticket + committee assignment placeholder. Reject: auto-sends rejection email with optional reason.05Bulk Actions"Select multiple delegates → Bulk approve, bulk reject, bulk export to Google Sheets. Filter by school, payment status, committee preference."06Waitlist ManagementAuto-fills approved spots from waitlist when a delegate cancels. Notifies next person on waitlist automatically.3.3 AI Portfolio Allotment FlowThe most painful part of running a MUN — manually matching 300 delegates to 40 committees considering preferences, experience, school caps, and conflicts. Freo MUN automates this with AI.01Input Phase"Registration closes. System has: all delegate profiles, committee preferences (ranked), experience level, school, any manual locks set by Sec-Gen."02Sec-Gen Sets Allotment Rules"Configure: max delegates per school per committee, preference weight (how much to honor 1st choice), experience distribution balance, manual locks (specific delegate → specific portfolio)."03AI Runs Allotment"Groq / Gemini API runs constraint-satisfaction optimization: maximize preference scores, enforce school caps, balance experience levels, detect conflicts (two delegates from same school in same committee)."04Review Allotment Output"Sec-Gen sees full allotment table. Stats shown: % got 1st preference, % got 2nd, conflicts found. Can manually override individual allotments."05Finalize & NotifyConfirm allotment. System sends each delegate their portfolio via email + updates their Freo MUN delegate dashboard.06Re-run if Needed"Cancellations after allotment? Re-run partial allotment for unfilled spots only, keeping confirmed allotments locked."3.4 Position Paper Management Flow01Sec-Gen Opens Submission WindowSets deadline per committee. Delegates can upload position papers (PDF/DOCX) through their dashboard.02Director / Chair ReviewsCommittee EB gets a review queue. For each paper: Accepted / Accepted with Comments / Rejected. Add inline comments visible to delegate.03Delegate Revises & Resubmits"If revision requested, delegate can resubmit. System tracks version history."04Position Paper ScoresOptional: EB can score papers (1–10) for Best Position Paper award tracking.3.5 Awards & Certificates Flow01EB Submits Award Nominations"A Marking management system like execl where marksheet can be created using custom fields and stuff or just upload the marksheet from execl files. Post-conference, each committee's Chair submits award picks: Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, Verbal Mention, Special Mention, Best Position Paper."02Sec-Gen Reviews & Approves"Cross-committee view. Confirm awards, resolve duplicates."03Auto-Generate Certificates"System auto-fills certificate template with: delegate name, conference name, committee, portfolio, award type, date, Sec-Gen signature field."04Bulk Download + EmailSec-Gen downloads all certificates as ZIP. Delegates receive their certificates via email + downloadable from their dashboard(optional) because mostly awards and certificates are given physically.05Participation CertificatesEvery delegate (awarded or not) gets a participation certificate automatically. No more manual bulk generation(optional).🎓 4. DELEGATE FLOWS4.1 Delegate Registration Flow01Discover ConferenceDelegate gets link from WhatsApp / Instagram / school notice board. This is the hardest flow to manage — Freo MUN handles it natively.Each JCC side has its own Chair, delegates, and speakers listCrisis Directors have cross-committee visibility — can inject updates to both sidesCross-committee directives: a delegate in Committee A sends an action that affects Committee BCrisis arc is shared — both committees progress through the same story stagesSec-Gen has a JCC overview dashboard — sees both rooms simultaneously🗄️ 7.
